Spaghetti

Description

A fitting recipe for some delicious spaghetti.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Ground Beef
  • Spices
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce

Steps

  1. Cook Spaghetti Noodles al dente.
  2. Brown ground beef and add tomato sauce and spices.
  3. Strain noodles and add meat sauce.
